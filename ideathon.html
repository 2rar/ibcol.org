HKBCS Ideathon

Prep, set, go!

8 workshops in 2 days! Join our virtual event: Learn more about blockchain solutions from blockchain experts in our Ideathon event.

Meet your teammates!

Network on Gather Town! HKBCOL Ideathon is the perfect opportunity for novice and veteran participants to network and form teams for upcoming blockchain olympiads. Our personalised Gather Town event offers the ideal interactive virtual experience for the most effective liaising.

    Who is it for?
  • July 23rd: Novice participants
  • July 24th: Both novice and veteran participants
Event Details
23 July
24 July
Saturday and Sunday 12:00 to 13.30 Hrs
  1. Venue: Gather Town Link: to be provided

Day 1
23 July
12:00 to 13.30 Hrs

Rudimentary workshops for novice participants and brainstorming session

Day 2
24 July
12:00 to 13.30 Hrs

Advanced workshops for both novice and veteran participants followed by open mic session.

Workshops
  1. Workshop 1: Design Thinking
    Topics to be discussed: Five step design thinking process, Incorporating design thinking model to devise blockchain challenges and solutions
  2. Workshop 2: Business/Lean Model Canvas
    Topics: Introduction to the lean model canvas, Examples of business models, Application
  3. Workshop 3: Blockchain Challenges Fundamentals
    Topics to be discussed: Problem Justification, Value Proposition, Alternatives
  4. Workshop 4: Blockchain Solution Design Overview
    Topics to be discussed: Solution Overview, Case Studies, Alternatives
Workshops
  1. Workshop 1: Blockchain Architecture I: Tokenization
    Topics of discussion: BTC, ICO, NFT, Assets, Tokenisation, design principles (Privacy, Consent, Trust, LOA)
  2. Workshop 2: Figma
    Topics of discussion: Prototype building and testing, Interface designing, Collaboration
  3. Workshop 3: Blockchain Architecture II: Identity & Data
    Topics of discussion: SSI, SCI, DID, CDI, VC, NFT revisited, design principles (Privacy, Consent, Trust, LOA)
  4. Workshop 4: Notion
    Topics of discussion: Project management, Board database block
Brainstorming Session
  1. Participants will integrate the workshop teachings and participate in real time discussions to conjure blockchain solutions to real world problems.
Open Mic Session
  1. Veteran participants are welcome to share their blockchain solution ideas with the mass audience to spark an intellectual conversation.
