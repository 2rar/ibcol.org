Micro-consulting Day

Business Pitch Simulation Workshop

Micro-consulting Day is a Business Pitch Simulation Workshop the HKBCOL pitch presentation. It is a virtual consultation event for students to pitch and obtain VIP (Value proposition, Inspiration and Presentation) feedback from HSBC subject experts on their proposal ideas and business pitching technique for skills improvement and to prepare for the official pitch in HKBCOL Finals.

Business Pitch Simulation Workshop

  1. HSBC sharing on business pitching techniques
  2. Breakout room pitch simulation with HSBC mentors (by appointment)
  3. Playback and hot tips for successful pitching

How to join?

  1. Register via the online form as a team of 1 - 6 by 4 Apr 2021
  2. Pick your preferred simulation timeslot(s)
  3. Submit a 200 words project brief to describe your problem statement and solution
  4. Prepare a 5 min pitch presentation for the private simulation session to obtain instant feedback and suggestions from HSBC mentors

*Only limited simulation slots are available, registration will be on a first register first-served basis.

Micro-consulting Day Agenda

Time Agenda Items
4:20 Guest reception
4:30 MC opening and speaker/mentors introduction
4:35 HSBC speaker sharing and Q&A
4:50 Pitch Simulation & Consultation (Break-out Rooms)
5:50 HSBC speaker closing
